About Me

I'm Alex Voss, a freelance Creative Director with over 12 years of experience helping companies build brands and digital experiences that truly stand out. My work lives at the intersection of strategy, aesthetics, and execution.

I've worked with global tech companies, venture-backed startups, and boutique studios — each project sharpens my instincts for what makes design not just beautiful, but effective.

My approach is simple: I care about the details that most people skip. The spacing, the motion, the typographic choices — these aren't afterthoughts. They're the difference between a brand that people forget and one they remember for years.

When I'm not designing, I'm usually exploring type foundries, collecting design books, or taking long walks that somehow always end up at a coffee shop.
